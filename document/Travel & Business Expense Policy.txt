YNLimited
Travel & Business Expense Policy
Effective Date: January 1, 2026
Version: 1.0
________________


1. Purpose
The purpose of this Travel & Business Expense Policy is to:
* Establish clear guidelines for official travel
* Define reimbursement rules and daily allowances
* Ensure responsible and cost-effective spending
* Standardize the expense claim submission process
All employees are expected to exercise good judgment and spend company funds responsibly.
________________


2. Scope
This policy applies to:
* All full-time and contractual employees
* All domestic business travel
* All travel approved by Department Head and HR
________________


3. Travel Authorization
Before making any travel arrangements:
1. Employee must obtain written approval from their Reporting Manager.
2. Travel details must be submitted via the Internal HR/Finance Portal.
3. Finance approval is required for trips exceeding 3 days.
Travel without prior approval may not be reimbursed.
________________


4. Domestic Travel Per Diem
4.1 Daily Allowance (Per Diem)
For approved domestic business travel, employees are entitled to:
* 3,000 PKR per day (Per Diem)
The Per Diem covers:
* Meals (breakfast, lunch, dinner)
* Local transportation (e.g., rickshaw, taxi within city)
* Minor incidental expenses
________________


4.2 Per Diem Rules
* Per Diem is calculated based on the number of approved travel days.
* Partial days (less than 8 hours) may be reimbursed at 50% (1,500 PKR).
* If meals are provided by the company or client, Per Diem may be reduced accordingly.
* No receipts are required for Per Diem claims.
________________


5. Hotel Accommodation Policy
5.1 Hotel Stay Limits
For domestic travel, hotel accommodation reimbursement limits are:
* Maximum 12,000 PKR per night (including taxes)
Any booking exceeding this limit requires prior written approval from the Department Head and Finance.
________________


5.2 Hotel Booking Guidelines
* Employees are encouraged to choose business-standard, cost-effective hotels.
* Luxury hotels are not permitted unless pre-approved.
* Itemized hotel invoices must be submitted for reimbursement.
Personal expenses such as:
* Mini-bar
* Laundry (unless trip exceeds 3 days)
* Spa services
* Room upgrades
will not be reimbursed.
________________


6. Air Travel Policy
6.1 Class of Travel
All employees must travel in:
* Economy Class only
Business or First Class travel is not permitted unless specifically approved in writing by the CEO.
________________


6.2 Booking Guidelines
* Flights should be booked at least 7 days in advance whenever possible.
* Employees should select the most economical fare that aligns with business needs.
* Flight changes due to personal reasons will not be reimbursed.
________________


7. Local Transportation
Reimbursable transportation includes:
* Ride-hailing services
* Taxi fares
* Public transport
* Airport transfers
Receipts are required for claims exceeding 1,000 PKR.
Use of personal vehicles may be reimbursed at a pre-approved mileage rate (if applicable).
________________


8. Non-Reimbursable Expenses
The following will not be reimbursed:
* Personal shopping
* Entertainment unrelated to business
* Traffic fines
* Alcoholic beverages (unless pre-approved for client events)
* Travel expenses for family members
________________


9. Expense Claim Submission Process
Employees must submit Expense Claims within 7 calendar days of completing their trip.
Failure to submit within 7 days may result in delayed or rejected reimbursement.
________________


9.1 How to Submit an Expense Claim
Step 1: Log in to the Internal HR/Finance Portal
Step 2: Navigate to “Expense Claim Submission”
Step 3: Enter travel details:
* Trip dates
* Purpose of travel
* Location visited
Step 4: Upload required supporting documents:
* Flight tickets
* Boarding passes
* Hotel invoices
* Transportation receipts
Step 5: Enter Per Diem calculation
Step 6: Submit for approval
________________


9.2 Approval Workflow
   1. Reporting Manager Review
   2. Finance Department Verification
   3. Final Processing by Accounts
Employees will receive email confirmation once the claim is approved or returned for correction.
________________


10. Reimbursement Timeline
Approved claims will be reimbursed:
   * Within 10–15 working days
   * Via bank transfer through payroll or separate payment
Incomplete claims may delay processing.
________________


11. Advances for Travel
Employees may request a travel advance for:
   * Air tickets
   * Hotel bookings
   * Long-duration trips (more than 3 days)
Unused advance amounts must be returned within 5 working days after trip completion.
________________


12. Policy Violations
Submitting false or inflated claims may result in:
   * Rejection of reimbursement
   * Disciplinary action
   * Recovery of funds
   * Termination (in severe cases)
________________


13. Policy Review
YNLimited reserves the right to amend this policy at any time to align with financial controls and operational requirements.
Employees will be notified of material changes through official communication.
________________


Acknowledgment
All employees are required to:
   * Read and understand this Travel & Business Expense Policy
   * Comply with submission timelines and documentation requirements
